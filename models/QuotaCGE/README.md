# QuotaCGE

Port of the import quota CGE model from Chapter 10.5 of:

Hosoe, N., Gasawa, K., and Hashimoto, H. (2004). *Handbook of Computable General Equilibrium Modeling*. University of Tokyo Press.

GAMS source: `quocge.280`.

Data:
- `sam_2_2.csv`

Equation dump is saved in `packages/JCGEExamples/models/QuotaCGE/equations.md`.
